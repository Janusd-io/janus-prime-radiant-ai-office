01 · context
The strategic frame

Why Singapore is the lead market.

Bonaventure committed on 12 May: Singapore is Janus's primary commercial market for AI Native. The frame matters because every team's priorities — content, infrastructure, monitoring, outreach — now route through this decision.

Three reasons it's Singapore, not UAE

  • The PM's May Day Rally speech (1 May 2026) framed two storms — Strait of Hormuz energy crisis + AI transformation — converging on the commercial built environment. Janus's pitch lands directly on that convergence.
  • UAE deprioritised for streaming-economy products — banking infrastructure isn't ready (batch-to-streaming gap, family-controlled banks).
  • Singapore is the Asia-Pac hub for REITs / SWFs / pension capital — and the regulatory anchor points (ISSB, MEI regime, carbon tax S$45/tonne, Tripartite Jobs Council) make AI-in-buildings a Government-aligned story.
