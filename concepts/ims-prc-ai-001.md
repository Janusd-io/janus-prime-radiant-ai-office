# IMS-PRC-AI-001 — Management of Internal AI Tools

The formal Integrated Management System procedure owned by [[simon-tarskih]] (ISO Lead) for managing internal AI tools at Janus, mapped to ISO 9001 Figure 1 process-elements logic (sources of inputs / inputs / activities / outputs / receivers / monitoring/control).

## Stages

9 numbered stages, 8 forms (F1-F8):

1. Request / Proposal (F1 — AI Tool Request Form)
2. Stage 1 — Intake & Triage with Gate 1 (F2)
3. Stage 2 — Technical Qualification with Gate 2 (F3)
4. Stage 3 — Sandbox & Domain Expert Evaluation with Gate 3 (F4)
5. Stage 4 — Approval Decision (F5)
6. IT Readiness / Deployment Prep (F6)
7. AI Tools Register Listing (F7)
8. Production Use / Implementation (training record)
9. Ongoing Review, Re-evaluation, Suspension or De-listing (F8)

## Alignment with Janus operational reality

Per [[puls-gap-analysis-vs-simon]] (2026-05-08), the operational equivalents at Janus are: Linear AIR as F7 register; `/ai-registry` skill comments as F2; `/ai-tool-evaluation` skill comments as F3+F4. Simon's stage 9 (re-evaluation / de-listing) is the documented gap to close.
