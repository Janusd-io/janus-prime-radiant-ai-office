# Add a recruitment tracker module to the HR dashboard

## Decision

A recruitment tracker will be added alongside the assessment module, consolidating CVs, status, interviews, scoring, and source per candidate in one view.

## Why

Theresa cannot currently see CV pipeline state across Slack, Excel, email, and personal drives. A single tracker with per-candidate one-line records plus stored CVs and assessment artefacts removes the search overhead and survives staff turnover.
