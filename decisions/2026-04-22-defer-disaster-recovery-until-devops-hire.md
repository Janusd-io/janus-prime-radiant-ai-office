# Defer multi-region disaster recovery until a DevOps hire is in place

## Decision

Cross-provider / offsite disaster recovery for production AI tools is deferred until the team has dedicated infrastructure staff.

## Why

Current sandbox state means a Hostinger outage is tolerable; daily VPS-to-Hostinger-storage backups exist. Building proper multi-region failover requires DevOps capacity the team doesn't yet have.

## Evidence

> Speaker 3: that's something we decided to kick that can down the road until we have an infrastructure person and staff.
