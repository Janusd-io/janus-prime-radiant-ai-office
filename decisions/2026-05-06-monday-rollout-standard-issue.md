# Monday.com will be rolled out company-wide as standard issue

## Decision

Monday.com will be deployed as standard issue to every department and every employee, unlike Deel / Xero / Airwallex which remain department-scoped.

## Why

Everyone in the company has tasks, so universal access removes a class of one-off approval requests. Onboarding cost is low because the AI Office will wrap Monday with thin clients (voice, Slack agent, per-department views) so end users do not need to learn Monday's UI directly.
