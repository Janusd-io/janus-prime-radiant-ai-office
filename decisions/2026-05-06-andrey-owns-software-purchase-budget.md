# Andrey is the budget owner for new software purchases

## Decision

When AI Office needs a new tool, the purchase request goes to Andrey rather than via individual company cards — no per-person software cards remain.

## Why

Existing per-user payment cards have been wound down; centralising software spend on Andrey keeps the spend trackable as the approved-tools register evolves and aligns with the request-channel (AI hub) flow Michael is pushing.
