# Commission London agency to redo brand guidelines (~GBP 20k)

## Decision

Andrew is putting in a proposal to a London agency at GBP 20,000 to redo the corporate brand guidelines (logo usage, Pantone colours, typography, photography, iconography, templates) on top of the existing Percegy work, with delivery in 4-5 weeks.

## Why

The existing Percegy guidelines are too thin (no Pantone values, no whitespace rules, no photography guidance), and Janus cannot ship consistent collateral, decks, or website without a proper rulebook. Doing the brand fundamentals via an external agency is a one-off worth paying for; ongoing capability work stays in-house.

## Evidence

> Speaker 1: I've put a proposal in. And I've got a cost element. It's 20,000 pounds sterling.
