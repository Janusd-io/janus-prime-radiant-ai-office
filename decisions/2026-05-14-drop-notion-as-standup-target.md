# Drop Notion as a standup target; transcripts go straight into Obsidian

## Decision

The standup skill stops pushing meeting outputs to Notion; transcripts and standup outputs are written directly into the Obsidian vault on GitHub.

## Why

Notion API has been hitting recurring errors, and the wiki is the synthesis layer anyway — keeping a parallel Notion surface only doubles maintenance.

## Evidence

> Speaker 3: Instead of Notion. I don't want to use Notion. I'm going to remove it. Speaker 2: We don't need Notion now.
