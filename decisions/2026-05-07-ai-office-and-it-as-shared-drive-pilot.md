# AI Office and IT Ops are the pilot departments for the Shared Drive convention

## Decision

AI Office and IT Ops will set up the per-department Shared Drive + Groups + restricted/public pattern first, as the worked example before the rollout is generalised to HR, Finance, Marketing, CEO Office, and Training.

## Why

AIO and IT have the operators with the deepest Google Workspace and IT-best-practice context; running it on themselves first surfaces the convention and naming pitfalls before it lands on departments who care less about the mechanics.
