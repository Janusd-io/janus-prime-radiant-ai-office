# Likely deprecation of Notion from the AIO operating stack

## Decision

Notion is on the way out of the AIO operating stack — the Prime Radiant replaces what Notion was doing, and the Claude-to-Notion MCP connection is slow and token-heavy.

## Why

Euclid stated directly that he is 'actually thinking of getting rid of Notion' and 'this replaces Notion'. Reasons given: the new markdown-on-Git substrate is far more efficient on tokens, the Notion MCP integration is slow, and the synthesis layer now lives in the wiki. The May-13 batch ingest demo showed Notion already being flagged 'out' in the live system. Treated as a directional decision rather than a final cutover plan.

## Evidence

> Euclid: I'm actually thinking of getting rid of Notion. I'm not sure if with this system, if we need Notion anymore. I think this replaces Notion.
