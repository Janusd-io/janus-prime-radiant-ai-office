# Slack remains leave-request front-end; Deel.com is back-end

## Decision

Keep Jehad's Slack-based leave form and approve/disapprove flow as the user-facing front-end; once Deel.com is live, sync approved leave to Deel as system of record rather than duplicating Deel's UI.

## Why

Janus is converging on Slack as the single request hub across departments, so forcing employees to also use Deel for leave would fragment the workflow; Deel keeps payroll/country-specific records while Slack handles mobile-friendly approvals.

## Evidence

> Theresa wong: I mean, for the leave request, if it's going to be working on deal, we link the same logic to deal. And the approve and disapprove will be from Slack.
