# IT/Ops Prime Radiant scopes to project-management first, separate from 'operations'

## Decision

The next Prime Radiant instance after AI Office and Marketing will be IT/Ops, scoped initially to project management (Rosa's facet) and treated as a distinct identity from the existing 'operations' (support) vocabulary; product comes later.

## Why

Rosa surfaced that the existing 'operations' department in the AIO vault maps to the support team, not her project-management work, which spans both internal standardised procedures and external client-facing delivery. Michael and Euclid agreed the IT/Ops side needs its own identity rather than being collapsed into operations, and that starting with project management (where Rosa already has organised materials) is the lowest-friction entry point. Product is in scope but deferred to a second pass.

## Evidence

> Euclid: We want to have an identity. What should we have? Rosa: Yes. I think we need to have the projects and also the products.
