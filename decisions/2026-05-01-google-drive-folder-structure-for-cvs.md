# Google Drive folder structure for CV storage

## Decision

Create a Recruitment folder in the existing HR Google Drive with sub-folders by branch (Dubai first, then Singapore), then by department, then by candidate name; the tracker auto-links to the candidate folder.

## Why

Gives a deterministic CV storage location that the dashboard can index, removes Slack/email scatter, and scales per branch and per department without forcing Theresa to manage 100s of loose folders manually.
