# AI Registry intake form: URL + submitter + timestamp + location only

## Decision

The Slack-based AI tool intake form captures URL, submitter name, timestamp and location — no approval step, no extra validation fields.

## Why

Bonaventure pushed for explicit fields including authentication/approval. Michael argued the cost of the activity is minimal and approval gates aren't needed at intake — the actual gating happens later at the evaluation stage. They closed on the minimal four-field set; duplicate-detection moves to the bot's first activity rather than the form.

## Evidence

> Bonaventure Wong: URL, the name of the submitter, and we can track it. ... Organization. And that's it. We don't need more than that.
