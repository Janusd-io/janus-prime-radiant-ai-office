# Evaluation gate is binary, four-of-four pass required

## Decision

The evaluation stage applies the AI policy as binary checks; a tool must pass all four hard criteria (e.g. doesn't train on our data, integrates with Google) to advance to sandbox.

## Why

Michael framed evaluation as the policy gate that's easy to automate precisely because it's binary, in contrast to the more informational enrichment pass. Sandbox is where subjective scoring happens.
