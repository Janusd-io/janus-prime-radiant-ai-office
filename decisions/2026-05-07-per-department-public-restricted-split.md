# Each department's Shared Drive has a restricted (internal) folder and a company-wide folder

## Decision

Each department drive will be split into two folders: a restricted one accessible only to that department's group, and a company-wide one (read-only to everyone) for materials like brand guidelines, employee handbook, etc.

## Why

Andrew's brand guidelines and templates need to be company-wide, but HR insurance policies and business licenses should not be. A single per-department drive with two clearly-named subfolders gives both surfaces without proliferating drives, and labels like 'HR' (open) and 'HR private/restricted' keep the convention legible.
