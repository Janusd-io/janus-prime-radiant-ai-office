# Package the ISO process generator as a reusable Claude skill for other departments

## Decision

Once Simon signs off on the AI Ops template, Jehad will convert it into a Claude skill that any department can drop in and adapt, with each department supplying its own Obsidian vault as the knowledge base.

## Why

A reusable skill scales the IMS rollout beyond AI Ops without each department having to recreate the structure from scratch. Departments tweak the base for their domain rather than starting blank, and the skill's quality depends on the requesting department first having a populated Obsidian vault.

## Evidence

> Speaker 3: This could be turned into a skill too, right? Yeah. I want to create a skill for other people to do it.
