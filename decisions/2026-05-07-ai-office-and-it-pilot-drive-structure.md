# AI Office and IT pilot the Shared Drive + groups structure first

## Decision

The AI Office and IT departments will set up the public/restricted Shared Drive + Google Groups structure as the worked example before rolling it out to HR, Finance, Marketing and the rest.

## Why

Both Jehad and Euclid have IT background and can validate best practice on a low-risk department before exposing other teams to a half-baked permission model. Michael has already created an AI Office folder with ISO and AI-procedure material that can serve as the seed.
