# Collapse personal vault into department vault

## Decision

Stop running a separate personal Prime Radiant vault per employee; each person works directly inside their department vault, which becomes the single source of truth.

## Why

The personal-vs-department two-tier model added confusion without adding content — when asked what would live in a personal vault that wouldn't live in the department vault, the answer was 'nothing'. Federation between the two tiers was not working in practice. A truly private home-machine vault is a separate concern. Single-tier keeps multi-user sync and curation tractable for the upcoming marketing, PM, and IT rollouts.

## Evidence

> Speaker 2: So personal becomes a department. So there's no separate personal and a department. A personal becomes a department.
