# Ignore Fireflies' own summary; use only the raw transcript

## Decision

The standup skill explicitly strips Fireflies' generated summary and works from the raw transcript only; Notion holds the canonical post-meeting summary built from the transcript.

## Why

All three attendees agreed Fireflies summaries are shallow, out-of-context and unreliable. Building summaries from the transcript with Notion as the running log gives compounded, context-aware memory.
