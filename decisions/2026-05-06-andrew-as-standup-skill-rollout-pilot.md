# Andrew is the formal standup-skill rollout pilot

**Decision date:** 2026-05-06
**Decided by:** Michael Bruck, Jehad Altoutou
**Source:** [[aio-2026-05-06]]

## What

Andrew (Marketing) is the formal first external rollout target for the standup skill. The previously-floated Lysander/Rosa group is deprioritised in favour of Andrew.

## Why

- Andrew explicitly asked for the skill — pull rather than push, which materially raises adoption odds.
- Rollout is partly chicken-and-egg with Monday's sandbox status: until Monday is in production, the skill's writes happen against a sandbox environment. Andrew is willing to start in sandbox; the Lysander/Rosa group would expect production.

## Implications

- Jehad will tailor and forward the standup skill for Andrew's marketing workstream this week.
- Andrew's adoption becomes the first stress test of "skill rolled out to a non-AIO user." Lessons from this rollout should be documented as `lessons/` pages.
- Lysander/Rosa rollout follows once Monday is in production and Andrew's pilot is running cleanly.
