# Google Shared Drives are the company-wide document store

## Decision

Janus will use Google Shared Drives (not personal My Drive folders) as the canonical per-department document store, with one Shared Drive per department.

## Why

Shared Drives are not owned by any individual, so files survive employee departures. Storing documents under personal accounts creates a migration burden whenever someone leaves. Permissions on Shared Drives can be inherited from Google Groups, which scales as headcount grows.

## Evidence

> Michael Bruck: First, you create shared drive. It does not belong to anyone. And then you let people give a people access.
