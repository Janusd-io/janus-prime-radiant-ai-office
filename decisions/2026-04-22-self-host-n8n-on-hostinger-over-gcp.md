# Self-host n8n on Hostinger VPS instead of GCP

## Decision

n8n is up and running on the Hostinger VPS and will be used going forward instead of GCP-hosted alternatives.

## Why

Cost analysis showed GCP would run ~$70/month basic and scale to $4-5K/year, vs. ~$900/year on Hostinger. More importantly, the team wanted to avoid the DevOps burden of AWS/GCP. Hostinger also exposes AI/agent control APIs.

## Evidence

> Speaker 1: If you want to use N8N, we also have N8N. It's up and running now.
