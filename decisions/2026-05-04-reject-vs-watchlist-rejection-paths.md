# Split rejection into hard-reject and watch-list with documented rationale

## Decision

Tools that fail evaluation are recorded in Linear in one of two categories: hard reject (with reason) or watch-list (revisit later, as was done with ChatGPT).

## Why

Bonaventure objected to 'reject because it's garbage' as undocumented; he required a documented reason and a distinction between 'never' and 'not yet'. Watch-list preserves optionality when a tool may become viable as vendor policy or product matures.

## Evidence

> Michael Bruck: We have actually two categories. We have one reject. Because it's a piece of shit. Two is keep an eye on it. Watch list.
