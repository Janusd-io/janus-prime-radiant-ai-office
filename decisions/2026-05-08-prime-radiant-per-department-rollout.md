# Roll Prime Radiant out per department

## Decision

Build a Prime Radiant instance for every department (starting Marketing with Andrew, then Operations/IT, then HR), with department folders as cross-department connecting nodes.

## Why

A single Obsidian-based knowledge graph already aggregates Fireflies, Monday, Linear, and Notion into vendors, decisions, lessons, projects, trends, and briefs. Department-scoped instances let each function (Andrew/Marketing first) act on their own data while shared department folders become the federation layer between teams, eventually rolling up to a board view.

## Evidence

> Michael Bruck: Every department will have a different version.
