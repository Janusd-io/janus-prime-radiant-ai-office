# Pilot the Fireflies-to-Monday-Notion-Linear knowledge pipeline with IT Ops first

## Decision

The AI Office's transcript-driven knowledge-management pipeline (Fireflies -> Claude skills -> Monday + Notion + Linear) will be cloned for the IT department as the first external pilot, with Jehad tailoring the skills, before being offered to other departments.

## Why

IT is the natural early adopter — Euclid and Andrey already juggle multiple workstreams (IT support, AI Office overlap, Zendesk), and Michael wants the same diary/run-log memory they have seen demonstrated. Starting with IT keeps the blast radius small and produces a second proof-point before company-wide rollout. The skills will not work out of the box because they hard-code AI-Office Monday boards, so Jehad will adapt them.
