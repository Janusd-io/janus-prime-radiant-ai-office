# Monday.com is not an AI tool and stays out of the AI registry

## Decision

Monday.com is classified as a general productivity / project-management tool, not an AI tool, and will not be tracked in the AI tool registry even though it ships AI features.

## Why

Every SaaS platform now markets AI features, so 'has AI' is not a useful registry criterion; the team will drive Monday via Claude agents externally rather than via Monday's native AI tier. Notion is treated the same way. This keeps the AI registry focused on tools whose AI capability is their primary function.
