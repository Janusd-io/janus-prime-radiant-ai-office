# Office of the CEO

Janus's executive office. Holds the company's mission, multi-year strategy, country expansion plans, and top-priority cross-departmental programmes. The Office of the CEO is the destination instance for the long-arc **digital knowledge twin** — federated visibility into what every other department's Prime Radiant has learned.

## People

- **[[bonaventure-wong]]** — CEO.
- **[[simon-tarskih]]** — ISO compliance programme lead, reporting to Bonaventure.

## Prime Radiant instance

Queued, but the framing matters: the Office of the CEO instance, when stood up, is intended to be the **federation endpoint** — the place where briefs and decisions from every other department's Prime Radiant flow up for executive synthesis. It's the closest thing to the eventual digital knowledge twin in instance form. Spin-up sequencing: deferred until at least three department instances (AIO, Marketing, plus one of HR/IT-Ops) are operating, so federation patterns are proven before the executive layer aggregates them.

## Infrastructure layer (per three-layer model)

This is where most of Janus's Infrastructure layer originates and is canonical:
- Mission statement
- Multi-year strategy
- Country expansion plans (SGP W19, GB W20, future markets)
- Annual operating plans and budgets
- ISO 9001 compliance framework and evidence (via [[simon-tarskih]] / [[iso-compliance-programme]])
- Top-priority programme charters

Other departments' Infrastructure layers (Marketing's mission framing, ITO's compliance evidence, Finance's budget envelopes) derive from the canonical versions held here.

## Key programmes

[[iso-compliance-programme]] — top-priority cross-departmental programme; Simon Tarskih lead under Bonaventure's sponsorship; multi-track scope (ISO docs, AIO tool-evaluation alignment, standup-skill alignment, ISO facilitation skill build).

## Cross-instance federation

Office of the CEO instance is the ultimate federation receiver — every other department instance feeds outputs upward for executive visibility. The mechanism is deferred until the multi-instance pattern matures.
