# IMS Process Register (41 processes)

The authoritative inventory of Janus Digital's IMS processes — **41 processes across 4 categories**, each with a proposed owner and ISO clause linkage. Basis for the documentation sprint. Source: [[reg-ims-process-register|REG-IMS-ProcessRegister v0.5]] (DRAFT, 2026-05-18; full table verbatim in the source). Owner of the register: [[simon-tarskih|Simon]] (Head of IMS & Compliance).

**Categories:** G = Governance (13) · C = Core (15) · S = Support (8) · M = Management (5). Owner status = Onboard / Hiring / Vacant; "interim" = who covers it today. Conflict resolution: two owner claims → escalate to Simon; no owner claim → escalate to [[bonaventure-wong|Bonaventure]].
