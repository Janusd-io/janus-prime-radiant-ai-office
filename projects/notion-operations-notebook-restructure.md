# Notion Operations Notebook restructure

The execution work behind the [[2026-05-06-notion-role-shift-journal-not-knowledge-base|2026-05-06 decision]] that shifted Notion's role from monolithic knowledge base to a narrower three-purpose surface: journal & meeting log, department landing pages, onboarding surface. AI-searchable knowledge migrated to Markdown + YAML (the [[janus-prime-radiant-build|Janus Prime Radiant]]).

## Scope

1. **Journal & meeting log surface** — daily AIO standup entries (`## AIO DD Mon YYYY`) appended by the [[standup|/standup]] skill; decision capture; next-step planning.
2. **Department landing pages** — segmented sub-pages for Simon/ISO, IT (Euclid/DA), HR (Rosa), Bonaventure (CEO), Andrew (Marketing).
3. **Onboarding surface** — org structure, team contacts, role mapping.

Everything outside these three buckets either moves to the Prime Radiant (canonical, AI-searchable knowledge) or stays in its system of record (Linear AIR for tool registry, Monday for execution tasks).

The journal / dept-landing-page work continues beyond that.
