# Reorder the deck's 'outputs' section: plans/campaigns first, then briefs/positioning, then POVs/white papers/assets

## Decision

The Prime Radiant deck's outputs panel will be reordered so plans and campaigns come first, briefs and positioning second, and POVs/thought leadership/white papers/assets last.

## Why

Current ordering reinforces an internal misconception that the marketing function is just a white-paper factory. Putting plans and campaigns first repositions marketing as strategy/execution and matches what Andrew was hired to do.

## Evidence

> Andrew Soane: I'd like to put plans and campaigns first. Briefs and positions, positioning second. Then POVs, thought leadership, white paper, and assets.
