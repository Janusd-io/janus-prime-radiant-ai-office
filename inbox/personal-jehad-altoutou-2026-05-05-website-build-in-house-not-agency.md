# Build the new Janus website in-house with Lovable + Claude, not an agency

## Decision

The Janus website will be built in-house using Lovable plus Claude-generated prompts rather than commissioned to an agency, once brand guidelines are fixed.

## Why

Michael has already proven the pattern with his own fractional-work site, where he used Claude to stack Lovable prompts to conserve tokens. Both agreed a website is 'a bunch of JavaScript' that AI is well-suited to generate, including interactive web apps not just static pages. Only the brand-identity work is being outsourced because that needs human design judgement.

## Evidence

> Speaker 2: Yeah. So I'm websites are fine. We can absolutely do it ourselves. Once we've got the rules in terms of what it looks like.
