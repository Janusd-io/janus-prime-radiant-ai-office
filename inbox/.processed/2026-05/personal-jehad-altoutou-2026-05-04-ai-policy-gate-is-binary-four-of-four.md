# AI policy evaluation gate is a 4-of-4 hard binary check

## Decision

To progress from Evaluating to Sandbox a tool must pass all four AI-policy criteria (training on our data, Google integration, security requirements, etc.); failure on any one is an immediate reject.

## Why

Michael described the evaluation stage as deliberately less subjective than the preliminary enrichment, tying it tightly to the approved AI policy so rejections are defensible and automatable. Most tools fail because vendors lack clear training-data policy, which is acceptable - the funnel is designed to be brutal early.

## Evidence

> Michael Bruck: I said binary. It's either - so it has to be check all four boxes to go to the next step.
