# Reject Fireflies summaries; ingest only raw transcripts

## Decision

The Prime Radiant ingest pipeline uses only raw Fireflies transcripts and ignores Fireflies' generated summaries because the summaries are insufficient and lossy.

## Why

The system has already captured this as a learning from prior meetings — summaries lose attribution and nuance, and the raw transcript is the only reliable source. Michael was emphatic about not consuming Fireflies' synthesis layer.

## Evidence

> Michael Bruck: Summaries are insufficient. We need the raw transcript to be right... That's why I never take a summary. It's garbage.
