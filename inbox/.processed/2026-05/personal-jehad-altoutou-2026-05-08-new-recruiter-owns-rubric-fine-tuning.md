# Incoming recruiter (starts next month) will own scorecard and JD fine-tuning

## Decision

The newly hired recruiter joining next month will take ownership of fine-tuning the CV scoring rubric, job descriptions, and the post-interview scoring loop — including investigating why candidates receive the scores they do.

## Why

The current rubric weights are subjective and were reverse-engineered by Jehad from one set of HR results. Michael wants curiosity built into the process — understanding why a candidate scores 5.5, not just accepting the number — so the engine compounds learnings over time rather than producing one-shot scores.

## Evidence

> Michael Bruck: His role is just going out there recruitment. But also, I want that him or the department is to fine-tune... the job description, how we're looking at people, the scorecard.
