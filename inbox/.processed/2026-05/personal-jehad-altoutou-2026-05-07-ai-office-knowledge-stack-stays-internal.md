# AI Office's Linear/Monday/Notion/Fireflies/Claude stack stays AI-Office-internal

## Decision

The AI Office knowledge stack (Linear = AI Arc registry; Monday = projects; Notion = log/notebook; Fireflies = capture; Claude Cowork = synthesis) is an internal management methodology and is not the company-wide knowledge surface for other departments.

## Why

Other departments need a document store and an internal portal staff can actually retrieve from, not an AI-Office-specific tool registry. The AI registry is the only AI-Office output that will be published outward; everything else in the stack supports AI Office's own operations.

## Evidence

> Michael Bruck: It's a combination of Fireflies, Linear, Notion, Claude. Yes. That's fine. That will not affect the general public.
