# ISO/IMS process documents follow Simon's standard structure: source, receiver, inputs, activities, controls, resources

## Decision

Every department process and sub-process document produced for the IMS will follow the same fixed structure — source, receiver, inputs, activities, controls, resources — rendered as a box-by-box diagram with text-described visuals.

## Why

Matching Simon's required documentation shape keeps the output ISO-compliant out of the box and makes the template reusable across departments. The text-described visual approach lets the AI regenerate the diagram deterministically from structured input rather than relying on image generation.

## Evidence

> Speaker 1: The source, receiver, inputs, activities, control, resources, and all of these. The same diagram.
