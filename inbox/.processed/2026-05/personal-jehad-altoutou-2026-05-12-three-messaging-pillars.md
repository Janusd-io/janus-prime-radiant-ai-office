# Three messaging pillars: SG sustainability, AI as growth engine, individual augmentation

## Decision

Janus marketing will pillar all content under three themes: SG sustainability/societal impact, AI as a business growth engine, and human augmentation/upskilling — with exact wording to be finalised.

## Why

A three-pillar structure gives navigation, content categorisation, and message coherence a stable spine. The third pillar (individual augmentation) explicitly carries the 'Uber for engineers' narrative Michael wants to keep alive. Wording is acknowledged as not yet locked.
