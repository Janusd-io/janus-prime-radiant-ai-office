# Prime Radiant vault lives on Janus Google Shared Drive, not personal Drive

## Decision

The Prime Radiant Obsidian vault is stored on the Janus Google Shared Drive so Janet and others have access, replacing the first-day iteration that lived in Michael's personal Google Drive.

## Why

Personal Drive sync worked but isolated the vault to one user. Shared Drive gives the EA and future department curators direct access, which is required for the federation model where every department gets its own instance. Sync mechanics are the same.

## Evidence

> Michael Bruck: It's sitting in a Google Drive... Google Share Drive. So Janet has access to it as well... We decided to share it.
