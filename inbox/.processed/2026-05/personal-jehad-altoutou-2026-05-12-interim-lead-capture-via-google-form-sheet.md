# Interim Singapore lead capture via Google Form into Google Sheets, pre-CRM

## Decision

Until the CRM lands, Singapore campaign leads will be captured via a Google Form on the gems.com/singapore single-page site, writing to a Google Sheet, which will later seed the CRM.

## Why

The Singapore lunch on 8-9 July is roughly 45-60 days away and cannot wait for CRM selection/implementation. A form-to-sheet pipeline is trivial to stand up, captures consent metadata, and the sheet content becomes the initial CRM seed once selection completes.
