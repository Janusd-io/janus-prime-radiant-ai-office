# Department heads, not IT, manage contributor membership on their drives

## Decision

Each department head will be the sole Shared Drive manager for their department, with the rest of the department added as contributors; the department head adds new contributors instead of routing requests to IT.

## Why

Routing every access request through IT will not scale once Janus has hundreds of folders. Contributors can add and modify files but cannot change permissions, which protects the structure. Managers in Google Drive can change permissions and add users, which suits a single department head.

## Evidence

> Michael Bruck: It's better to assign the main, the manager of the department as a manager, and he adds contributors. It's the easiest way.
