# Frame Janus marketing around five capabilities plus CRM and website

## Decision

The marketing function will be structured around five named capabilities — competitive analysis, brand voice, formatting/visual, ICP and messaging, social outreach — with CRM and website/CMS as adjacent buckets, so processes built today scale from a function-of-one to a larger team.

## Why

Andrew is starting as a function-of-one and wants to bake AI-native processes into the foundation before scaling. Naming the capabilities lets Michael map each to skills/projects/tooling. The capabilities are explicitly designed to not change as the team grows from 1 to 10 to (potentially) more.
