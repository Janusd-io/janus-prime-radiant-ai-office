# Janus must adopt a CRM despite the long-term SaaS-replacement vision

## Decision

Janus will adopt a CRM in the near term as the system of record for marketing and sales activity, even though Bonaventure's three-year vision is to phase out external SaaS.

## Why

Andrew argued he cannot run effective marketing without a CRM hub, and Michael agreed the long-term in-house replacement is two to three years out, which is too long to wait. Both noted CRM is strictly more important than Deel (back-office) for a company that needs customers first. Michael will surface this back to Bonaventure who has gone cooler on the idea.

## Evidence

> Speaker 1: Personally, I think we absolutely have to have a CRM. ... Speaker 2: Yeah. I agree.
