# Adopt three messaging pillars: society, business, individual

## Decision

Janus messaging will pillar up to three themes: driving the sustainability/SG agenda (society), AI as growth engine (business/capital), and augmenting/upskilling people (individual).

## Why

Three-pillar framing gives content, navigation, and messaging a coherent spine. Includes the 'Uber for engineers' angle under the individual pillar.
