# Scope five core marketing capabilities for AI build-out

## Decision

The marketing function will be built around five named capabilities: competitive analysis, brand voice, formatting/visual brand, ICP and messaging (with a message-house artefact), and LinkedIn/X social outreach.

## Why

Andrew explicitly framed these as durable processes that survive the team scaling from one to ten to a hundred. Michael agreed they form the right unit of work and pushed back on the word 'skill', settling on 'capability' as the canonical term so they map cleanly to the AIO playbook.

## Evidence

> Speaker 2: So that's sort of I'm not going to call it skill. I'm going to call it output one. Capability.
