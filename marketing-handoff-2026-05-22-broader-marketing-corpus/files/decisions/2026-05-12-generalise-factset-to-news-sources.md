# Drop FactSet name from the deck; generalise to 'news sources / information gathering'

## Decision

FactSet will be removed from the inputs slide and replaced with a generic 'news sources / information gathering' category, with LinkedIn flagged as the biggest single intelligence source.

## Why

Calling out FactSet by name overweights one Bonaventure suggestion and risks defining the strategy around it (same trap as the white-paper framing). The biggest signal source will be LinkedIn (and Twitter), and there will be hundreds of sources — single-vendor naming is wrong at this level of the plan.
