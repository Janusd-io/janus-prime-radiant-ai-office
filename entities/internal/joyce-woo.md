## Background

40+ years of corporate and private banking experience across Singapore and Southeast Asia. Senior roles spanning credit, operations, compliance, risk management, investment management, marketing, and sales. Source: [[2026-05-14-janus-singapore-white-paper-storms-ahead|Janus Singapore white paper]] "About the authors" section.

Career timeline:

- **1982** — Started banking career as **Credit and Marketing Officer at DBS Finance**.
- **~12 years at Merrill Lynch International Bank** — regional management positions across banking operations, compliance, and marketing/sales.
- **2001** — Joined **OCBC Private Bank** to establish its investment advisory team. Also served on the investment committee for OCBC Trustee Limited.
- Senior client relationship roles at **Citigroup, UBS, Morgan Stanley, Bank of Singapore**.
- **2014** — Founded **Jachin Capital**, a Singapore MAS-licensed fund manager.
- **2023** — Jachin Capital acquired by **Leo Capital Corporation**, rebranded as **Leo Wealth Singapore**.
- **Until 2026** — **Chief Executive of Leo Wealth Singapore.**
- **2026** — **CEO, Janus Digital Singapore Pte. Ltd.**

Education: **Bachelor of Business Administration, National University of Singapore**.
