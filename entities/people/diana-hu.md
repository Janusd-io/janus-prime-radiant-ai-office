# Diana Hu

**Role:** Partner at Y Combinator
**Organisation:** Y Combinator (YC)

Diana Hu is a YC Partner whose framing of AI-native companies is load-bearing in the [[recursive-self-improving-loop]] concept and the [[organisational-digital-twin]] thesis at Janus.

## Why she's in this wiki

Her *"AI as Operating System"* formulation and the closed-loop architectural framing she articulated in the YC talk *"Building an AI-Native Company From the Ground Up"* (2026-04-24) are cited as the YC-partner-level external validation for what the AIO is building. Specifically:

- **AI as OS:** treating AI not as a productivity tool for individuals but as the coordination substrate of an organisation — AI handles the communication and coordination layer that middle managers currently own.
- **Closed-loop:** the self-improving loop (data → AI action → output → data) is the architectural primitive that makes the organisational-digital-twin pattern compound over time.

Her framing aligns with Tom Blomfield's five-part articulation (see [[tom-blomfield]]) and together they form the YC-partner consensus layer that the [[recursive-self-improving-loop]] concept is built on.
