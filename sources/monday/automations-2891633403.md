## Context

Cowork is in active use across the AI Office team but has not been formally approved through the AI Policy gate. This is a governance gap that needs closing before Cowork is used for sensitive workstreams or rolled out beyond the AI Office.

## Definition of done

- Cowork passes the binary 4-of-4 AI-policy gate (data training, security, integrations, ...)
- Approval recorded in Linear AIR with approver name
- AI Policy doc updated to list Cowork as approved
- Usage scope documented (who, for what, with which data classes)

Note: This is governance-side work, not a tool evaluation. Cowork's capabilities are well known; the friction is documentation and approval.
