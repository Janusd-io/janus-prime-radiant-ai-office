Counter-narrative to agent hype. Critical analysis of productivity claims. Argues that many organizations overhype agent ROI without accounting for: integration complexity, context window limits, hallucination costs, human oversight overhead, and the friction of moving from demo to production. Questions whether "autonomous agent loops" deliver on promises or simply move the problem elsewhere (now debugging agent failures instead of fixing bugs directly).
