Campaign objectives

Establish Janus Digital Singapore as a credible, named institutional voice in the Singapore real estate and AI conversation, anchored to the white paper as the foundation for all subsequent commercial activity.

Position Joyce Woo as the recognised face of Janus Digital in Singapore, through coordinated leadership announcements, founder-led outreach, and direct engagement with senior institutional contacts.

Generate 60–120 qualified, consent-confirmed leads from across REITs, family offices, asset/fund management firms, and institutional real estate operators in Singapore.

Deliver a high-quality thought leadership luncheon on 8/9 July with 8–12 C-suite institutional executives from the target sectors, building direct relationships with the decision-makers Janus needs to influence.

Secure earned media coverage in Business Times, The Edge, or equivalent Singapore institutional publication to multiply the credibility of the white paper and the launch.

Build a base of warm pipeline conversations to support a defined commercial pipeline for the second half of 2026, converting campaign-generated relationships into qualified opportunities over the following six months.

Establish the operational foundations for a sustained Singapore marketing programme, including lead capture infrastructure, audience intelligence, CRM-ready data, and proven channel effectiveness to support ongoing pipeline nurturing through 2026 and beyond.
