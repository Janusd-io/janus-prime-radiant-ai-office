## About Janus Digital

Janus Digital is a technology company with global operations based in the UAE, delivering Autonomous Intelligence for Real Assets. The company provides an enterprise-grade, AI-powered platform that functions as the central operating system for intelligent buildings, transforming how real-world assets are managed, optimised, and operated worldwide.

Janus Digital was established to acquire and internationalise our Intelligent Building Operating System — a mature, fourth-generation technology platform built on over 15 years of dedicated research and development and more than $100 million in invested R&D capital alone.

## Mission

Janus was founded with the conviction that artificial intelligence should serve a greater purpose than mere efficiency. Buildings account for nearly 40% of global energy consumption and carbon emissions, yet most remain managed by outdated, fragmented systems that react to problems rather than prevent them. Janus exists to change that — proving that AI for Good is also good business.

## The Technology: An MRI for Buildings

Think of Janus as an MRI for buildings and critical infrastructure. The platform creates millimetre-accurate digital twins of entire structures — every pipe, wire, and duct — and continuously monitors, in real time, how energy, water, and air flow through every system.

Traditional building management relies on reactive alerts, siloed systems, and manual optimisation. Janus replaces this entirely with:

- **Predictive Intelligence:** Anticipates maintenance needs days in advance, preventing failures before they happen
- **Unified Dashboard:** Integrates all building systems into one intelligent view for complete visibility
- **Autonomous AI Optimisation:** Physics-grounded AI making thousands of micro-adjustments 24/7

Unlike language-based AI models that can hallucinate, Janus is built on physics — thermodynamics, pressure dynamics, energy conservation, and fluid mechanics. The platform is powered by **HGTFT (Heterogeneous Graph Temporal Fusion Transformer)**, a proprietary forecasting framework that combines Transformer layers for temporal pattern recognition with Graph Attention layers for modelling physical relationships — the result: **91% improvement in physical realism** compared to standard models. Janus calculates — it doesn't guess.

## Technology Heritage & Global Platform

Janus Digital was established in Dubai as a purpose-built vehicle for global expansion, housing intellectual property in a common-law jurisdiction that provides robust IP protection, transparent governance, and international enforceability. The company was architected from day one as an AI-native organisation, positioning Janus Digital to scale rapidly across **10 countries in 2026**.

**The Results: Tangible Impact**

- ~29% Energy Cost Savings: AI-driven efficiency begins immediately and grows as the system learns
- ~29% Reduced Staffing Needs: Automated optimisation frees teams to focus on high-value tasks
- ~28% Less Equipment Downtime: Predictive maintenance prevents system failures before they happen

## Strategic Context

This role sits within the AI Office, the team responsible for designing, building, and running Janus Digital's AI platform and the engineering capability behind it. Reporting to the Head of AI Office, the engineer joins a small, senior group working at the core of how the company applies artificial intelligence across its global operations.

The platform is accessed through multiple surfaces — Slack, web dashboards, and purpose-built interfaces — by users ranging from AI-native engineers to operations staff who have never opened a terminal.

The platform is architected around composable microservices — discrete, independently deployable services owned by small squads as the team grows.

## Key Responsibilities

### Full-Stack Product Engineering
- Build and own the product across the stack: backend services and APIs in Python and TypeScript; frontend interfaces in React for web dashboards, administrative surfaces, and AI agent interaction layers.
- Own features end to end — design, implementation, testing, deployment, and operational health.

### Cloud, Infrastructure & Reliability
- Build cloud-native software on AWS (primary).
- Containerisation (Docker, Kubernetes) as the standard deployment unit.
- Engineer for reliability: observability (logging, metrics, tracing), graceful degradation, sensible SLOs.
- Partner with the CTO and infrastructure team on deployment and platform reliability.

### Enterprise Identity, Security & Access Control
- Enterprise-grade authentication and authorisation: SSO (SAML/OIDC), SCIM provisioning, RBAC, fine-grained IAM.
- Regional compliance: GDPR, Singapore PDPA, UAE data protection frameworks.
- Zero-trust principles, least privilege, defence in depth throughout.

### Platform Architecture & Multi-Tenancy
- Design the platform for multi-tenancy from the ground up: configuration isolation, provisioning flows, usage metering.
- Architect for global distribution — latency, regional data residency, multi-timezone support.

### AI Agent Development & Orchestration
- Build and deploy production AI agents using **MCP and streaming APIs**.
- Design **multi-model orchestration across Claude, Gemini, and other models** as the landscape evolves.
- Manage system prompts, API boundaries, and context controls across model providers.

### Memory & Knowledge Systems
- Contribute to and evolve the **memory and knowledge architecture at the core of the platform — a structured, multi-graph, file-based system underpinning institutional intelligence across the company**. This is among the hardest and most consequential engineering problems on the platform.
- Build the integrations between the memory system and agent workflows so agents consistently access the right context regardless of which model or interface surface is in use.
- Stay current on the rapidly evolving field of AI memory and context management; evaluate emerging approaches and adapt the architecture as better patterns emerge.

### Microservices Architecture
- Architect the platform as a composition of isolated, independently deployable microservices — each functional domain (finance tools, marketing stack, knowledge layer, agent runtime) a discrete service with well-defined APIs between them.

## Profile

Strongest candidates come from environments where software failure has real consequences — a digital bank, a payments or fintech company, or a product-focused engineering firm building regulated financial systems. 5+ years full-stack production experience required.

## Location

Dubai, UAE (Global Operations) with travel to regional offices (Singapore, Turkey, Spain, UK) as required (15–25%).
