# Internal branding — Prime Radiant vs Nomi vs brain vs wiki, sub-names consolidation

## The question

Multiple names are in active use for what is, structurally, one initiative or a closely-related family of initiatives:

- **Janus Prime Radiant** — the system-level proper noun ([[janus-prime-radiant-build|programme hub]]); locked at the title level (CLAUDE.md v0.7+).
- **Nomi** — a name that exists in circulation; per the standup, [[bonaventure-wong|Bonaventure]] is *not* keen on it.
- **Brain** / **Digital Brain** — the supporting metaphor used for non-technical audiences when introducing the system; [[andrew-soane|Andrew]] resonated with this framing in his brainstorm.
- **Wiki** / **LLM Wiki** — the technical methodology name ([[llm-wiki]]); used internally with engineers; sourced from Karpathy.
- **Janus Pulse** — working title for the **onboarding skill** (owned by [[michael-bruck]]) that bootstraps an individual into the personal-vault pattern (separate artefact, but the name overlaps the others).
- **PULS** — *Predictive Unified Live System*; the branding (owned by [[jehad-altoutou]]) for the ISO programme deliverable (process docs + dashboard + template-per-entity). Distinct from Janus Pulse the skill but easy to confuse.

This proliferation was flagged in the 11 May 2026 standup: *"I think we need to have a conversation with Andrew about internal branding. We've got all these sub-names happening. Like, Nomi, which Bonaventure is not keen on."*

## What's already partially resolved

Per [[2026-05-08-wiki-vs-brain-metaphor-by-audience|the 2026-05-08 lesson]]: the system *name* is fixed ("Janus Prime Radiant"); the *supporting metaphor* varies by audience — engineers hear "wiki", non-technical hear "brain". This is a partial resolution; it doesn't address Nomi, Pulse (skill), PULS (ISO programme), or other sub-names that are circulating.

## What still needs deciding

- **Nomi** — is this name retired (Bonaventure's preference), or does it serve a distinct purpose worth preserving (e.g. a non-Janus-branded consumer surface)?
- **Janus Pulse** (skill) vs **PULS** (ISO programme) — homophone confusion already happening in conversation. Disambiguate at the name level, or accept the overlap and rely on context?
- **Brain / Digital Brain** — formalise this as the canonical non-technical metaphor (per the existing lesson), or pull back if it creates confusion with personal-vault-as-brain framing?
- **Sub-instance handles** — *Janus Prime Radiant · Marketing*, *· HR*, etc. are the formal handles per CLAUDE.md §1. Should each instance also have an internal short-hand (e.g., "Marketing Brain"), or is the formal handle plus context sufficient?

## Stakeholders

- [[andrew-soane|Andrew]] (CMO) — the standup surfaced that Andrew should be involved given his marketing/branding accountability; he's the natural owner for an internal-naming decision.
- [[bonaventure-wong|Bonaventure]] (CEO) — has already taken a position against Nomi; final say if a name reaches the company-wide stage.
- [[jehad-altoutou|Jehad]] and [[michael-bruck|Michael]] — participants in the meeting that surfaced this. Michael is current owner of this question (per frontmatter) pending Andrew involvement.

## Proposed next step

Schedule a focused conversation with Andrew on internal-branding consolidation. Goal: rationalise the active name set, retire what should be retired, agree the audience-mapping for what survives, and capture the decision in `decisions/`.

This is a non-urgent but accumulating ambiguity; the longer multiple names circulate, the harder consolidation becomes.
