# Emma McCall

**Role:** Head of Certification/Training at Janus Digital. **Start date:** 29 June 2026. Comes from a large corporate environment with highly restricted AI usage; new to AI tools but strong domain expertise in training and certification design.

Emma's mandate: build and run the Janus certification programme, initially targeting BMS (Building Management System) engineers for the HGTFT/DTX platform. Later scope: internal Janus staff training + external market certification programmes. See [[bms-engineer-certification]] for the project hub.

## Domain expertise

Adult learning design: known → unknown progression, audience analysis (fear vs excitement spectrum), dual-mode learning (on-demand retrieval vs structured skill acquisition), continuous checkpoint assessment, gamification and progress tracking.

Her opening question at first meeting: *"What do we want them to be able to understand and do by the end of the learning, and to what quality level?"* — framed the certification programme correctly from the start.
