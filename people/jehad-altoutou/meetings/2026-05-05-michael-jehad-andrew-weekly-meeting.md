## Summary

Michael walked Andrew (new marketing leader, function-of-one) through LLM fundamentals — context windows, artifacts vs Gemini canvases, projects, Markdown-as-lowest-common-denominator, the Claude PowerPoint/Word/Excel plugins, and why Claude leads on coding/harnesses while Gemini leads on deep research and image/video gen. HubSpot, Attio, Monday CRM, and Salesforce are the candidates to benchmark. Andrew also pushed back on Notebook LM presentation output as 'cringy' and unbranded — to be banned for external-facing decks. Standing operational follow-ups: set up a Claude project for AI marketing, a shared Google Drive folder, and a three-person Slack channel.
