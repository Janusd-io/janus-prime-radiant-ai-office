## Slide 15

[[puls-first-voice|PULS First Voice]] — your input shapes the system

To build a system that reflects how we work, we need input from everyone. Your answers become the foundation of PULS — your processes, your KPIs, your tools.

After this session you will receive PULS First Voice — a short form, 10–15 minutes. Your answers are used to define your process, responsibilities and KPIs inside PULS.

1

Your work — inputs & outputs

What do you need to do your work? What do you produce and who receives it? This defines your process in PULS.

Sources of inputs

Internal departments and predecessor processes;

external parties — clients, partners, suppliers, regulators

Inputs

Information, tasks, requests, documents,

requirements and resources needed to perform the work

Outputs

Work results: completed tasks, documents,

decisions, services or products passed to the next recipient

Receivers of outputs

Internal teams or departments receiving the result;

external parties — clients, partners, regulators

2

Controls and check points

Points within the process where activities are checked for conformity with established requirements and output quality is assessed before handover to the next participant

3

Tools & systems you use

Every tool in your daily work — AI tools, software, platforms. This feeds our AI systems register.

4

Your KPIs — how you measure success

What does good performance look like in your role? Your view shapes how PULS monitors your process.

5

What you need to work better

IT access, information, training, support from other teams, clarity on responsibilities.

6

Your questions & ideas

What do you want to know about PULS? What concerns you? What ideas do you have? Every answer goes into PULS.

What happens with your answers

- Your process document is drafted using your input — you review and confirm it
- Your KPI suggestions are included in Performance Monitoring
