About Janus Digital
Company Overview
Janus Digital is a technology company with global operations based in the UAE, delivering
Autonomous Intelligence for Real Assets. The company provides an enterprise-grade,
AI-powered platform that functions as the central operating system for intelligent buildings,
transforming how real-world assets are managed, optimised, and operated worldwide.

Mission
Janus was founded with the conviction that artificial intelligence should serve a greater purpose
than mere efficiency. Buildings account for nearly 40% of global energy consumption and
carbon emissions, yet most remain managed by outdated, fragmented systems that react to
problems rather than prevent them. Janus exists to change that—proving that AI for Good is
also good business.

Position Overview
Janus Digital is transitioning to an AI-native operating model across 50+ global markets. We are
looking for a hands-on AI Operations & Enterprise Platform Engineer to own the enterprise
Slack platform and the AI operations infrastructure that powers the company.
This is not a research role. This is an operational engineering role for a ‘coder-administrator’
who can connect systems, build automation scripts, architect secure integrations, and test new
AI tools to determine whether they actually work. You will act as the engine room of the AI
Office: clearing the backlog of tools to evaluate, securing our Slack and Google infrastructure,
and writing the glue code that makes our systems talk to each other.
Reports to the Head of Infrastructure with a dotted line to the Head of AI Office.
