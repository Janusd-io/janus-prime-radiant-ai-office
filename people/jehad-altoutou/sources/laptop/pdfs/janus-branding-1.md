Janus logo design

JANUS Branding

【Visual Elements Analysis】

A doorway-like passage, with the central black doorframe designed as a
stylized transformation of the letter "J".

The yellow block on the left represents the physical world, while the blue
block on the right of the door suggests the virtual realm.

The design conveys a strong sense of depth and dimensionality, paired
with a modern, tech-forward visual identity.

The typeface is a rounded geometric sans-serif with a clean and futuristic
aesthetic.




【Symbolic Interpretation】

The “door” is the most iconic symbol associated with Janus, who is
revered as the God of Doors and Gates.

The door represents beginnings and endings, choices and transitions,
and the connection between two worlds (e.g., physical and digital).

The open state of the door signifies future possibilities, exploration, and
the embrace of change, radiating a sense of positive energy and
transformation.
