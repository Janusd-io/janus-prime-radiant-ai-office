# Gartner formalises "Digital Twin of an Organization Platforms" Magic Quadrant — Sep + Nov 2026

Per [[odt-competitive-analysis-2026]]: Gartner has scheduled dedicated **Magic Quadrant for Digital Twin of an Organization Platforms** sessions at two flagship 2026 events:

- **Gartner Application Innovation & Business Solutions Summit, London — Monday 14 September 2026**
- **Gartner IT Symposium/Xpo, Barcelona — Wednesday 11 November 2026**

This is the formal analyst confirmation that the DTO space has crossed from "emerging technology hype cycle" into "named, evaluable market category." Gartner's definition: *a dynamic software platform that relies on operational and contextual data to understand how an organization operationalises its business model, connects with its current state, responds to changes, deploys resources, simulates future states, and delivers customer value.*

## Platforms evaluated

The report names four in the Gartner DTO category (others may be added by the time the MQ publishes):

- **Mavim iMprove** — integrates process mining with strategic execution; ESG + operational excellence focus; deployed at Renewi. Source: [mavim.com/dto](https://www.mavim.com/dto).
- **QualiWare** — dynamic enterprise "flight simulators" in 8–12 weeks; Gartner "Tech Leader" designation. Source: [qualiware.com/digital-twin-of-an-organization](https://www.qualiware.com/digital-twin-of-an-organization).
- **Corporater** — GRC + strategy execution platform; maps risk universes; automates compliance monitoring. Source: [corporater.com/platform/digital-twin-of-an-organization](https://corporater.com/platform/digital-twin-of-an-organization).
- **Interfacing EPC** — Enterprise Process Center; FDA, ISO 9001, GDPR compliance focus; Gartner-listed since at least 2023. Source: [interfacing.com/gartner-digital-twin-dto-2023](https://interfacing.com/gartner-digital-twin-dto-2023).

None of these platforms are in Janus's current AI Registry. They are established enterprise-architecture / process-mining vendors that have converged on the DTO label — not AI-native startups.

## Why this matters for Janus

1. **The MQ publication date (Sep–Nov 2026) is the window** during which enterprise buyers will begin evaluating vendors against a formal Gartner framework. Any Janus external positioning around "organisational digital twin" or "AI-native operating model" should be timed to arrive before or alongside the MQ coverage, not after.

2. **The Gartner definition validates the [[organisational-digital-twin|AIO's concept framing]]** almost verbatim — the wiki's "continuously-updated structured model that supports queries, simulations, and predictions" matches Gartner's "responds to changes, simulates future states, delivers customer value." Janus already has a vocabulary and substrate that fits the analyst-named category.

3. **The current MQ field (Mavim, QualiWare, Corporater, Interfacing) is legacy process-mining and enterprise-architecture players**, not AI-native builders. The "AI-native company world model" that Block, Cloudflare, YC, and Anthropic are articulating is *a different, newer entrant type* in the same category. The field is not yet settled — first-mover advantage for an AI-native DTO player is real.

4. **HGTFT's physical-twin lineage** is cited as the primary Janus competitive strength (per [[odt-competitive-analysis-2026]]): *"Janus can bypass the technical debt of legacy players and run a lean, highly leveraged AI-native operating model."*
