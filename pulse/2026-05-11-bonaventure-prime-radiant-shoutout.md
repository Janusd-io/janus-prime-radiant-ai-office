# Bonaventure shout-out on Prime Radiant — happening sooner than expected

In the 11 May 2026 AIO standup, it was mentioned (offhandedly) that [[bonaventure-wong|Bonaventure]] had given a positive shout-out on the company-wide knowledge-base work and is impressed it's progressing sooner than expected. The recap surfaced in the meeting: *"We got a nice shout-out from Bonaventure. I think he's quite impressed with what we've built here. And where it's going. It's actually happening quicker than I thought."*

## Signal

Bonaventure is Janus's CEO and the executive sponsor for both the [[iso-compliance-programme|ISO programme]] and (implicitly) the [[janus-prime-radiant-build|Prime Radiant rollout]] as a company-level deliverable. A positive but informal signal from him at this stage is meaningful for two reasons:

1. **Validates the pace.** The Prime Radiant pattern was greenlit on 2026-05-05; the [[marketing-prime-radiant|Marketing instance]] is kicking off 2026-05-08; Bonaventure is reading the cadence as faster than he expected. That removes pressure to accelerate further and gives room for the AIO instance to keep honing in parallel with the Marketing pilot.
2. **Pre-empts political resistance.** When the rollout reaches HR, Finance, IT-Ops, and Office-of-CEO instances, executive backing is the single biggest enabler. An early shout-out — even informal — is the leading indicator that backing is forming.

## What this is not

- Not a formal decision or commitment. Bonaventure has not allocated budget, scoped a company-wide initiative, or made a public announcement.
- Not a measure of how the Prime Radiant pattern is performing on its merits. It's a stakeholder-confidence signal, not an outcome signal.
- Not load-bearing for the rollout plan — the rollout proceeds on the merits per [[janus-prime-radiant-build]], with or without leadership praise.

## Watch for

- Bonaventure's framing when he refers to Prime Radiant in subsequent standups, town halls, or strategic conversations. Does he use the formal name? Does he reach for the brain metaphor? Per the meeting: *"Bonaventure gets that 'Prime Radiant' is very geeky. He's Welsh by nature. He knows that's... he got it right away."*
- Whether the praise leads to executive ask — e.g. a request for a leadership-visible aggregation view across instances. That would be the trigger to design the company-level digital-twin layer beyond the lightweight mesh federation pattern.
