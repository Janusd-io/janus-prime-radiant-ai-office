## Process note

This is the first lint that executed *from* a prior lint's carry-forward queue rather than from a fresh structural scan. The pattern worked well — most session effort went to durable improvement (frontmatter backfill, attribution-rule cleanup, question retirement) rather than re-discovering known issues. Memory entry [[project-lint-workstream-backlog]] documents the convention for future Claude sessions: read the previous lint's "Carry-forward" section first, execute, then scan for net-new findings on top.

The carry-forward pattern proved valuable enough that **future lints should always end with a "Carry-forward" section** even when the report is otherwise clean. Codify in CLAUDE.md §5.3 at next schema bump.
