Topic Context What I'd like
What do we call this system, in plain language? Inside the AI Office we call it Janus Prime Radiant. For non-technical audiences across the firm — including new departments coming online and the people whose names go on outputs from the system — that name needs softening. Bonaventure's read was "very geeky." The system name stays; the user-facing label is open. Andrew should be in this conversation. A twenty-minute decision on the call.
Are we on the right pace, or should we accelerate? Three departments live by end of May if Project Management lands cleanly. HR, Finance, ISO, and the Office of the CEO are next. Bonaventure's 11 May read was that the work is happening faster than he expected — calibration question: compress the queue further, or hold pace? A read on cadence from the CEO.
How forcefully do we land the Notion sunset? End-of-May target is locked from the AI Office side. Departments still using Notion as a system of record after that date need to move. The AI Office can run the migration, but the move-or-lose signal needs to come from leadership. Confirmation, and who relays it to department heads.
When does the "personal vault" feature come back? The original vision included a personal vault for every employee that feeds into their department's vault. We've parked that — it works for one vault, breaks down at two — and team-level vaults are progressing in the meantime. We want a timing call: revisit next month, next quarter, or treat as a longer-term roadmap item? A timing call.
